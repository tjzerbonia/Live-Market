CSV Import Guide

How to format your spreadsheet before importing markets.

The basics

One spreadsheet handles both prediction markets and sportsbook bets. The first column tells the importer which type each row is:

First column valueWhat it creates
sportsbookA Book Bets market (moneyline, spread, or total)
anything elseA regular prediction market (YES/NO or multi-option)

The header row is required. You can mix both types in the same file.

American odds — read this first

American odds are just a way of saying how much you win for every $100 you bet. There are only two types: negative and positive.

-150

Negative = the favorite.

You have to risk MORE than $100 to win $100.

-150 means: bet $150, win $100 profit.

Think of it as: the minus sign tells you what you have to put in to get $100 back out.

+130

Positive = the underdog.

You risk $100 and win MORE than $100.

+130 means: bet $100, win $130 profit.

Think of it as: the plus sign tells you what you get back for every $100 you put in.

The one number you'll use constantly

-110

This is called standard juice (or "the vig"). It's the default for almost every spread and total bet. Both sides get -110.

It means: bet $110 to win $100. The house keeps $10 as their cut.

When in doubt, use -110 on both sides.

Quick cheat sheet

OddsWhat it means in plain EnglishBet $100, you win...
-200Heavy favorite. You're almost expected to win.$50 profit
-150Solid favorite.$67 profit
-110Standard juice. Slight edge to the house.$91 profit
+100Perfectly even. No favorite.$100 profit
+130Mild underdog.$130 profit
+200Big underdog. Unlikely to win.$200 profit
+500Major longshot.$500 profit

The one rule to never forget

Negative numbers always need the minus sign. If you type 150 instead of -150, the importer thinks it's a +150 underdog, not a -150 favorite. That's the most common mistake.

Common mistakes

MistakeFix
Odds written as 150 instead of -150Always include the minus sign for favorites
Odds written as +110 when you mean -110Standard juice is negative: -110
Sportsbook with capital SMust be lowercase: sportsbook
Subtype written as Over/UnderMust be exactly total
Probabilities don't add up to 100Prediction market rows are skipped if probs don't sum to 100
Missing the header rowFirst row must always be the column names
Total odds entered in the wrong columnsFor totals: Line in col K, Over in col L, Under in col M
